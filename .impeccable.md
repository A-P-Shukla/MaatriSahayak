## Design Context

### Users
Primary users include district health officers, ambulance drivers, government stakeholders, NGOs, donors, and the general public. Public pages must build trust, explain impact, and guide users to the right portal.

### Brand Personality
Compassionate, trustworthy, authoritative.

### Aesthetic Direction
Purple/rose health-tech palette with a refined government-portal feel. Light-first UI with optional dark mode for comfort. Avoid flashy startup vibes; prefer dignified, credible, and human-centered visuals. Use strong typography, generous spacing, and clear calls to action.

### Design Principles
- Trust first: prioritize clarity, credibility, and verification cues.
- Human impact: show outcomes and real-world urgency.
- Guided entry: role-based navigation with low cognitive load.
- Accessible by default: WCAG AA contrast and reduced-motion support.
- Consistent tone: calm, professional, and compassionate.
